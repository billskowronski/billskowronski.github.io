Case Study — Rome Grinding Solutions

Reframing a Fourth-Generation Manufacturer’s Story to Rebuild Pride and Win Bigger Opportunities

Snapshot

  • Role: Brand strategist, executive comms partner, lead writer
  • Challenge: Morale collapse, leadership transition, fragmented story
  • Audience: Employees, customers/prospects, industry influencers
  • Deliverables: Message house, brand narrative, website copy, sales deck, trade show messaging, employee stories, crisis comms

Approach

  • Inside-out discovery: interviewed employees, captured pride/proof points; co-created content to build buy-in.
  • Message architecture: clarified narrative and value prop framework to align leadership and sales.
  • Human-first storytelling: shifted from features to a people-powered partner story.
  • Omnichannel rollout: translated narrative to web, sales enablement, and booth.

Outcomes

  • Executive alignment: CEO reaction: “We’ve been trying to say this for years. You got us.”
  • Employee pride & retention: employees now proudly share the website and enjoy being featured in marketing materials.
  • Pipeline quality: sales reported stronger confidence and significantly larger opportunities.

Case Study — Optimas Solutions

From Disparate Resources to a Global Knowledge Engine

Snapshot

  • Role: Initiative owner & lead strategist for the Resource Center; owner of the “Better Together” campaign
  • Challenge: Scattered technical content limited search performance and sales enablement
  • Audience: Global engineering, procurement, and operations stakeholders
  • Deliverables: IA & taxonomy, SEO roadmap, content briefs, governance, analytics; LinkedIn campaign creative & copy

Approach

  • Resource Center build: centralized content with clear taxonomy, metadata, and faceted discovery; led cross-functional execution.
  • Search & content strategy: emphasized high-intent topics and robust technical guides.
  • Sales integration: designed for quick-find sharing during prospect conversations.
  • Campaign activation: launched “Better Together” to raise awareness and drive qualified traffic.

Outcomes

  • Visibility growth: appeared in 2.5M Google searches, a ~700% YoY increase.
  • Global utilization: technical materials accessed worldwide in multiple languages.
  • Demand signals: notable jump in contact form submissions from prospective customers.
  • Sales adoption: Resource Center became the go-to hub for reps to share relevant content.
  • Campaign results: 6,265 impressions · 127 clicks · 2.03% CTR (vs ~0.49% benchmark) · $1.26 CPC (vs ~$5.58) · 3% engagement (vs ~1.4%).

Source for visibility and demand metrics: internal performance summaries (Q1 2025).
